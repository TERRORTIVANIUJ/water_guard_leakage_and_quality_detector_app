About

Water stress plagues areas all across South Africa, and each area experiences water issues caused to different degrees by excessive use, growing demand, pollution, theft, thirsty plants, inadequate infrastructure, climate, and poor practices. South Africa is a Water scarce country, receiving only half the precipitation of the global average. With this said, it is important for us to safeguard the integrity of our Water supply hence this application was created to provide a solution to limit the wastage of water in and at the same time managing water quality in supply pipelines. Our application seeks to provide a platform within which municipal governing bodies or contracted water companies are alerted in the case of leakage detection and quality monitoring for speedy maintenance, reducing losses.

Features

Water Leakage

The detection of water pipeline leakage is important to ensure that water supply networks can operate safely and conserve water resources. Causes of water pipeline leakage include and not limited to the corrosive nature of the soil, deficient pipe material quality, the temperature and pressure, failure to employ standard pipe laying methods, geological changes, and human damage. Our system will determine any apparent leakages using sensor network installed at various segments of the pipeline system, measuring any deficiencies in water pressure and flow rate of the supply system.

Water Quality

Water quality monitoring aims to provide the data required for safeguarding the water supply system against adverse biological effects from multiple chemical contamination arising from anthropogenic diffuse emissions and point sources. In this case, our system seeks to monitor water quality sensors that determine the pH levels contained in the system, the turbidity of the water, monitoring chemical constituent trends and screening of any impairments detected, alerting users of any discrepancies in the water quality supply system.
